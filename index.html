Our Story

The Garden Bistro was founded by two Sober folks who wanted a chill spot to hang out with friends without the booze. Picture a cozy garden vibe that's upscale but without the fancy wine glasses. That's us.

We started this place because we craved a place where people could relax, enjoy each other's company, and have a good time without the alcohol. No frills, just good food, refreshing mocktails, and a laid-back atmosphere.
