The Garden
Bistro

Step into The Garden Bistro, where we're all about good vibes
and great food. Our place is alcohol-free, but that doesn't mean
we skimp on flavor. Sip on refreshing mocktails, infused with herbs
and florals, as you soak in the chill vibes of our cozy garden landscape.
Our menu has something for everyone — vegan, gluten-free,
and veggie options that taste as good as they make you feel.
